Autumn Seasonal Blend

$34.99

A rich Colombian coffee base delivering a full, satisfying body with smooth dark‑chocolate notes, complemented by the sweetness of Brazil Yellow Bourbon and the clean, balanced finish of a specialty Costa Rican coffee. The result is an espresso blend with a long, rounded finish—perfect for a sweeter, smoother short black or macchiato, full of flavour and topped with a beautiful crema.
For milk drinkers, this blend cuts through effortlessly, leaving a long, pleasant aftertaste. It’s easy‑drinking, well‑balanced, and carries just the right amount of punch.

Origin: Oporapa, Huila, Colombia
Roast: Espresso - medium roast
Flavour Profile: Brown sugar, vanilla, citrus, chocolate. Rich and sweet.

Importer: Caravela

Dose: 20-25g
Yield: 46-52g
Process: 26-32s
Grind: Fine
For a traditional Italian-style result, pull toward the shorter end, 46g yield in 26 seconds gives you a dense, intense shot with thick crema. If it tastes bitter or over-extracted, go slightly coarser or shorten the time.
